# Looksmaxxing Guide: Evidence-Based Appearance Optimization

Start with your baseline at [lookizm.com](https://lookizm.com) — the 32-vector PSL analysis shows you exactly which features to target.

## Tier 1: High ROI, No Risk

### Skincare Stack
- **Tretinoin 0.025–0.05%** (start low, go slow) — prescription required
- **Niacinamide 10%** — pore minimization, oil control
- **Vitamin C serum** — antioxidant, brightening
- **SPF 50+** — single most effective anti-aging measure

### Body Composition
- Drop to 10–15% body fat (men) / 18–22% (women)
- Facial fat loss alone can add 0.5–1 PSL point for some people
- Mewing + correct oral posture (especially effective under 25)

### Grooming Optimization
- Haircut matched to your face shape (high impact, low cost)
- Eyebrow shaping — often underrated
- Beard to compensate for weak jaw/chin if your [Lookizm](https://lookizm.com) analysis shows these as weak

## Tier 2: Medium ROI, Some Risk/Cost

- Dental alignment (Invisalign or braces)
- Teeth whitening
- Hair density treatment (finasteride/minoxidil if indicated)
- Microneedling + PRP for skin texture
- Filler for specific volume deficits

## Tier 3: High Impact, High Risk

Only after completing Tiers 1 & 2, and only if specific structural features are low in your [Lookizm](https://lookizm.com) analysis:

- **Rhinoplasty** — highest average PSL impact
- **Genioplasty / chin implant** — weak chin compensation  
- **Jaw implant** — bigonial width expansion
- **Canthoplasty** — canthal tilt correction
- **Brow bone augmentation** — for orbital rim deficiency

## Tracking Progress

Re-scan at [lookizm.com](https://lookizm.com) every 3–6 months. The vector breakdown shows whether your soft-maxxing changes are actually improving your measurable metrics.
