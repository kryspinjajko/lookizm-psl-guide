# The 32 Facial Vectors Analyzed by Lookizm AI

[Lookizm.com](https://lookizm.com) analyzes each of these vectors when you upload your photo:

## Eye Region (8 vectors)
1. **Canthal tilt** — angle of eye corners relative to horizontal
2. **Orbital depth** — how recessed the eyes appear
3. **Eye width** — absolute and relative to face width
4. **Interpupillary distance** — distance between pupils relative to face width
5. **Eye height** — vertical open height of eye
6. **Lid crease position** — double vs mono lid, crease height
7. **Brow-eye distance** — vertical distance from brow to eyelid
8. **Scleral show** — visible white below iris

## Midface (7 vectors)
9. **Midface ratio** — nose-to-lip distance as fraction of total face
10. **Nasal bridge height** — forward projection of nose bridge
11. **Nasal width** — width relative to interpupillary distance
12. **Nostril shape** — flare ratio
13. **Philtrum length** — nose base to lip top distance
14. **Lip fullness** — upper/lower lip ratio and overall volume
15. **Lip width** — relative to nose width and face width

## Lower Face (8 vectors)
16. **Jawline definition** — angle sharpness and continuity
17. **Jaw width** — bigonial width relative to bizygomatic
18. **Chin projection** — forward and vertical
19. **Chin shape** — square, pointed, or cleft variations
20. **Mandibular angle** — gonial angle measure
21. **Ramus height** — vertical ramus height
22. **Lower facial third ratio** — relative to mid/upper thirds
23. **Neck-jaw angle** — cervicomental angle

## Symmetry (5 vectors)
24. **Horizontal symmetry** — left/right across vertical axis
25. **Eye level** — height differential between eyes
26. **Mouth corner level** — height differential
27. **Ear position** — symmetry where visible
28. **Nasal deviation** — deviation from facial midline

## Proportions & Ratios (4 vectors)
29. **Face length/width ratio** — overall face shape
30. **Golden ratio adherence** — deviation from φ across key distances
31. **Neoteny index** — composite of neotenic features
32. **Sexual dimorphism score** — gender-appropriate feature strength
